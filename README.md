# SAMP Version Spoofer R5

## EN

This script allows you to spoof your SA:MP client version to **0.3.7-R5**. It utilizes a helper library and MoonLoader to manipulate server-side checks.

### Installation

1. Place `spoofer.lua` directly into your `moonloader` folder.
2. Place `R5Helper.dll` directly into your `moonloader` folder.
3. Restart your game. The script will initialize automatically upon connecting to a server.

### Compatibility

Tested on Balkan servers. Functionality on Russian or custom-launcher-based servers is not guaranteed as they may employ different validation methods. Use at your own risk.
